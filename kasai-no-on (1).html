The Origin

Every scar tells a story. Every flame hides a name. This is the legend of Kasai no On.

— Chapter 01
Before the Fire

Born in a world where silence was forbidden, Kasai was trained from birth to become a weapon of the shadow council. Silver hair, no name — only a number. The tattoos on his skin were not chosen; they were branded, each rune binding a piece of his power.

— Chapter 02
The Red Awakening

The day his eyes turned crimson was the day the council lost control. The runes burned gold, then red — and Kasai walked out of the compound leaving nothing but ash and one message carved into the gate: 炎の音. The Sound of Fire.

— Chapter 03
The Stream Dimension

Now he exists between worlds, streaming from a pocket dimension of his own making. He appears on screens across the globe — part demon, part legend, fully unhinged. His viewers are his army. His merch is his sigil. Wear it, and the flame finds you.
